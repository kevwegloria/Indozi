About us

Indozi connects you to vetted and trusted workforce to carry out your daily demanding tasks. With Indozi, you can post any task you need to be done, online or offline, remote or local, and receive bids from qualified taskers within minutes. All taskers on Indozi are vetted and background-checked, so you can be confident you're getting quality work on a budget without compromise.
